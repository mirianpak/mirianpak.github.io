Films and Series

Hansel & Gretel (2013): watched 1/24/2014

You'll be going on a witch hunting journey. I didn't feel compelled to watch this movie when it came out in theaters or when I could purchase it through the Apple store. I finally got myself to watch it with Netflix today. I must say it was entertaining - like Harry Potter was entertaining. The acting was fairly good - except for the Good White Witch's which was mediocre/bland at best. She didn't have such a big part to spoil it. My recommendation: watch with little expectation and be entertained for 87 minutes.
